About Our School

GBHSS Manjeri has been a beacon of quality education for over five decades, shaping future leaders and responsible citizens.

Our Legacy of Excellence

GBHSS Manjeri (Government Boys Higher Secondary School, Manjeri), established in 1998, is one of the oldest and most prestigious educational institutions in Malappuram district, Kerala. Located near Court Road, Manjeri, the school is a vibrant center of learning that blends tradition with modernity. With classes from 11 and 12, it provides quality education under the Kerala State Board, nurturing more than 2800+ students guided by a dedicated team of teachers. The campus boasts spacious classrooms, computer labs, science labs, and a vast playground that supports both academic and co-curricular growth. Over the decades, GBHSS Manjeri has earned a reputation for discipline, excellence, and holistic development, inspiring generations of students to achieve success with knowledge, values, and confidence.
