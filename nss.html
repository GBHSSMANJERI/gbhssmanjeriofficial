About NSS

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a student-centered program aimed at developing the personality of students through community service. NSS volunteers work towards the upliftment of society, promoting national integration, and building a sense of social responsibility. Our unit strives to uphold the motto "Not Me But You" through impactful projects and outreach programs.

Activities & Events

  • Environmental awareness campaigns and tree planting drives.
  • Health camps and blood donation programs.
  • Cleanliness and hygiene awareness under Swachh Bharat Abhiyan.
  • Workshops on leadership and community development.
  • Participation in national and state-level NSS camps.
